Composition Techniques

Rule of Thirds

Divide your frame into 3x3 grid and place key elements along the lines or intersections to create balance and interest.

Leading Lines

Use natural lines to draw the viewer’s eye into the scene, toward the subject, or along a path.

Framing

Surround your subject with elements that create a frame (like windows, arches) to focus attention and add depth.

Symmetry

Use symmetrical compositions to create harmony and beauty, especially with architectural or reflective subjects.
