Smartphone Photography Skills

Clean Your Lens & Use Grid

Always clean your phone camera lens before shooting to remove fingerprints and smudges. Enable grid lines in settings to align elements using the rule of thirds for better balance.

Tap to Focus & Adjust Exposure

Tap on your subject to focus accurately. Use the brightness slider that appears to adjust exposure manually for optimal lighting.

Find Good Light

Natural light is key. Shoot during golden hour (early morning or late afternoon) or use shaded areas to avoid harsh shadows and overexposure.

Stability & Composition

Hold your phone steady with both hands or use a tripod. Compose thoughtfully—include leading lines, symmetry, or frames within frames to make photos more compelling.
